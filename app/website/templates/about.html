ℹ️ About This Tool

Welcome to the CV Classifier & Skill Advisor — a smart tool designed to help university students understand how their CVs align with different career paths and identify areas for improvement.

🤖 Powered by BERT: Why It Matters

This tool uses BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers), a cutting-edge machine learning model developed by Google, to classify your CV with a high degree of accuracy.

🔍 What is BERT?

BERT is a Natural Language Processing (NLP) model that understands the context and meaning of words in a sentence — not just individual terms. This makes it much more effective than older models at interpreting the real content of your CV.

🧠 Why BERT for CV Classification?

  • Understands the context of your skills and experience.
  • Can classify your CV even if the job title isn’t explicitly mentioned.
  • Handles complex sentence structures and real-world writing styles.
  • Offers state-of-the-art accuracy in text classification tasks.

📊 What Happens After Classification?

Once your CV is classified into a job category:

  • Your key skills are extracted using NLP.
  • You receive a skill report and recommendations to help you prepare for those roles.

🔐 Your Privacy

  • CVs are not stored after processing.
  • No personal information is saved or shared.
  • This tool is intended for educational and self-improvement purposes only.

🛠️ Built With:

  • BERT (HuggingFace Transformers) for classification
  • spaCy for skill extraction
  • Flask for the backend
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend

Designed and developed as part of a university research project on AI in career guidance.
